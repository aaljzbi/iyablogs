Valorant Addiction

Games, June 24, 2021

It's been a few weeks since I started playing Valorant. Even though I wasn't the best player around, it was the fact that playing with friends made it super fun hence I can't seem to stop myself from playing. Communication between players is very important and through this game, I was able to build relationships with people I didn't think I'd get to know in a face-to-face setting due to the distance and all that. I think it's really cool how games could make people connect despite the distance and spend time with them through one's interests.
